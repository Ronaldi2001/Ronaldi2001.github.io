About Ronnie Williams


My name is Ronnie Williams, my birthday is April 8th, 2001. I lived in Cranston until I was 8, then moved to Coventry and started first grade. I graduated from Coventry High School in 2019 and now majoring in Accounting in the class of 2023. I am the Assistant Manager at Auntie Anne's in the Warwick Mall and a Shift Leader at Dunkin' in Coventry. After working for four years I saved enough money and recently I purchased a 2020 Jeep Grand Cherokee, this is one of my dream cars. I plan on having this car till the end of its life then upgrading to a Tesla model X, my other dream car. When I graduate college I plan on opening a restaurant with my close friend. As you can tell I like working in the food industry why would I major in Accounting? My future plan is to own a few businesses and do all the accounting work which is something I love and at the same time I can also work in a restaurant which I also love. As working as management roles for the past four years I've learned a lot of good things to do and a lot of bad things not to do. I plan on using my findings of how to be a good management role model in the future when I have a business of my own!
